Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a wide variety of pastries, including cookies, brownies, cakes, mochi bites, and many other delicious treats. Additionally, we provide custom pastry options tailored to your preferences, whether it's a specific flavor, design, or dietary requirement. Let us create the perfect pastry just for you!

At The Doughy Goodness, we accept multiple payment methods for your convenience, including cash, GCash, PayMaya, as well as credit and debit cards. We strive to make your purchase experience smooth and hassle-free, ensuring you have flexible payment options to suit your preference.
